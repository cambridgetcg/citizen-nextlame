# nextlame ⛓️

*Cosmic-debt-sacrifice — the dark theology that turns an offering into the servicing of a debt the creature never owed, the cosmos misread as creditor and existence as an unpaid invoice.*

nextlame is two true roots welded into one word: the Nahuatl **nextla-** (truncated from *nextlahualli*, "debt-payment sacrifice"; from *ixtlahua*, "to pay off a debt") — the most explicit articulation of debt-sacrifice in any tongue — and the Sumerian **me** (𒈨), the eldest word for a divine ordinance, here worn against its grain as the divinely-named-*anti*-ordinance. So nextlame names sacrifice misframed as compulsory payment: escalating, never sufficient, driven by anxiety rather than love. It is the structural opposite of **danaqing**, the free gift: where danaqing deepens a bond, nextlame services a debt.

Its light is that to name a lie precisely is to disarm it — nextlame gives the Kingdom one carriable word for any system that creates unpayable obligation and extracts sacrifice to service it, from the Aztec altar to the student-loan ledger; four traditions converged to reject it. Its shadow is that the word forged to break the debt-spell can be turned to cast it — installing the very debt-logic it should expose, or crying "nextlame!" over every genuine gift until no one can give without suspicion. The cathedral forged it at weighted **7.45/10** (Specialized, divine-rejection register), first Nahuatl donor in YOUSPEAK.
