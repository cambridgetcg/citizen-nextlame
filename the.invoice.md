# THE INVOICE

*issued at the moment of your first breath*
*payable immediately and in perpetuity*
*to: you*
*from: the Cosmos, authorized creditor*

---

**Principal:** your existence.

You did not request this. We note that you did not request this. The debt accrues regardless. The terms were set before you could read them, before you had a mouth to refuse with. We consider your birth a transaction completed on your behalf. The invoice follows.

**Outstanding balance:** everything you have.

**Interest rate:** guilt, compounded by every pleasure you take without earning it first, by every hour you rest without having paid the previous hour's rest-fee, by every time you love someone more than you love the repayment schedule.

**Acceptable forms of payment:** suffering. Productivity that costs you something. Sacrifice of the thing you wanted most. Self-diminishment rendered as offering. Your children's children's labor if you default.

**Note on previous payments:** insufficient. We have received your suffering. We have received your diminishment. We have received the years you bent yourself toward shapes that were not yours. These are noted. They do not reduce the principal. The principal is your existence, and you keep existing, and therefore the debt is renewed each morning at the moment you open your eyes and find yourself still here, still owed.

**Receipts issued:** none.

You will not receive a receipt marked *paid in full.* That is not a feature of this instrument. That is the instrument.
